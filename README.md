## Project Overview

TerraBloom (stylized as **raBloom** in the navigation) is an environmental awareness and conservation platform focused on protecting terrestrial life, combating deforestation, addressing the wildlife extinction crisis, and promoting reforestation and biodiversity preservation.

The website raises awareness about pressing issues such as:

- Deforestation in the Amazon
- Rapid wildlife extinction driven by habitat destruction and climate change
- Urgent need for stronger conservation efforts

It encourages community participation through education, volunteering, events, and donations.

**Tagline:**  
*Together We Bloom, Protecting Every Life on Land.*
