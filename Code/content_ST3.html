Reforestation

Every tree counts; every action matters!!

Why Reforestation Matters

  • Carbon Sequestration: Trees absorb CO‚ÇÇ, mitigating climate change.
  • Biodiversity Conservation: Restored forests provide habitats for wildlife.
  • Water Cycle Regulation: Forests influence rainfall patterns and prevent erosion.
  • Soil Protection: Trees stabilize soil and improve fertility.
  • Community Benefits: Supports local economies and sustainable forestry.

Key Strategies for Reforestation

Various approaches are used to restore forests effectively:

  • Afforestation & Reforestation: Planting trees in deforested areas.
  • Agroforestry: Integrating trees with crops for sustainable land use.
  • Assisted Natural Regeneration: Allowing forests to regrow naturally.
  • Urban Reforestation: Creating green spaces in cities.
  • Rewilding: Letting nature regenerate with minimal human interference.
